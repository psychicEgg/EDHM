# No FID Mod v1.102.1 for Elite Dangerous 4.2.0.3

### by Fred89210<br><br>

**-- NEW for Elite Dangerous 4.2.0.3 --**<br><br>

**Release Notes:**
- Fixed an issue where the FID was not hidden in certain situations

-------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Description:**

You often share your screen and want to keep your Frontier ID (FID) private, this 3PMod is for you.

This 3PMod allows you to hide your FID displayed at the Bottom Left of the Main Menu.

If you encounter any issues with this mod, please contact me on the [EDHM Discord](https://discord.gg/KTYgJegfYw).<br>

-------------------------------------------------------------------------

**How to use:**

With EDHM_UI:

Go to `Main Menu -> 3PMods -> No FID Mod` and Show/Hide your FID or change the Key.<br><br>

Without EDHM_UI:

Edit `NoFIDMode.ini` and change the line `global $ShowFID = 1` or `Key = 0x8f`.
